About us

Who are we?

The AnimaPet NGO is an organization that provides instructions on how to raise and adopt a pet. AnimaPet promotes pet adoption events and collects donations to support animals living in shelters or on the streets. Our goal is to: Answer questions about adoptions and donations, promote NGOs and websites that seek to help abandoned animals, share news about pets, and instruct our readers on how to take care of their pets.

Adoption

Why adopt a pet?

If you wish to adopt a pet and contribute to our work of continuing to rescue animals, please schedule a visit to our institution. Whether you want to adopt or simply spend time showing affection to the animals, feel free to contact us through your preferred method. Most of the time, we rescue animals that have been abandoned or have suffered mistreatment. As a result, they tend to be much more affectionate with their owners, developing a strong sense of loyalty and gratitude. We appreciate your interest and support. Thank you in advance.
